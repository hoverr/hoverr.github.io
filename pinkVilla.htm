EXCLUSIVE: Deepika Padukone Ranveer Singh Wedding: INSIDE details of all night lavish party by the newlyweds

Post their twin wedding ceremonies; newly married couple Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh will be hosting an all-night lavish party for the guests at Lake Como in Italy.
Written By Aishwarya V 75296 reads Mumbai Published: November 16, 2018 12:50 am
  • 1
The celebrations are not over for Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh at Lake Como in Italy. After having twin wedding ceremonies, the newlyweds are in the mood to let their hair down and party all night. Yes, after Anand Karaj ceremony held today, the couple will be hosting a lavish party for their intimate guests celebrating their life as Mr and Mrs Bhavnani. This lavish party will be their last night in Italy as the guests will soon start returning back home.
 
Meanwhile, Deepika and Ranveer with their families will be coming back to Mumbai on November 18, 2018. Talking about the lavish party, the couple has gone all out and about 50 special cocktails will be served to the guests. The food has been relishing in these many days and we can expect the same for the after party as well. We just wish to be a fly in that room and witness all the amazing moments DeepVeer are having right now.
 

Ranveer and Deepika have dated for six years before getting hitched and its beautiful to see this love story reach its final stage of marriage. They have time and again given us couple goals. Their love story began on the sets of Sanjay Leela Bhansali's Goliyon Ki Rasleela - Ram Leela making them the ultimate Ram Leela of Bollywood. 

 
Deepika and Ranveer will soon be heading to India where they will make their first public appearance as a married couple at the Bengaluru reception which is scheduled for November 21. The couple will then host two receptions in Mumbai on November 28 and December 1, respectively. The November 28 reception will see a host of invitees, but Bollywood will be seen blessing the couple on December 1.

EXCLUSIVE: Ranveer Singhs sister Ritika has a surprise planned for Deepika Padukone & Singh when they return

Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh got hitched in Italy. Ranveers sister Ritika planned a great surprise for them.
Written By Bhavna Agarwal Mumbai Updated: November 16, 2018 04:19 pm
  • 0
Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh are finally man and wife and their union has only reinforced our belief in eternal love. The couple who dated for six years and fell in love on the sets of Ram Leela got hitched in a South Indian Konkani wedding on November 14, 2018 followed by a North Indian Sindhi wedding on November 15, 2018 at Lake Como, Italy. The couple will be returning to Mumbai on 18th November, and then, they will fly to Bengaluru for the November 21 reception which will be attended by a few family members and friends. 
 
As the couple got hitched in Italy, Ranveers abode in Mumbai was decorated beautifully to celebrate the union of Bollywoods Ram Leela. To know about it, we got in touch with Wedding Cupids who did the decor for them, and they revealed exclusively to us that it was Ranveer Singhs sister Ritika Bhavnani who was the brain behind this as she wanted to surprise her brother. Now, we found this to be too cute. Sahib Singh, the CEO and founder of WC told us, We have been given these decor specification by the family (Ranveers Dad Mr Jugjeet Bhavnani and Sister Ritika Bhavnani) as it was supposed to be a surprise for them. We have customised this entire decor as per the likes of the family members. The brief given was pretty clear, classy and elegant.
 
We hope the couple enjoys the surprise! 
 
Meanwhile, the couple is currently looking for a new abode in Mumbai. Till then, the actor will be moving in with Deepika in her house. The couple will host two receptions in Mumbai, first on November 28 and next in December 1 where Bollywood friends will be invited. Anushka Sharma, Katrina Kaif and Priyanka Chopra among others are invited for the reception in Mumbai. 
 
Heres wishing the couple a very happy married life! 

Is Hailey Baldwin supportive of Justin Bieber's decision to take a break from making new music?

Justin Bieber has decided to take a break from making new music and is instead focused on spending time with his wife, Hailey Baldwin. Read further to know if Hailey is supportive of her husband's decision.
Written By Karishma Shetty Mumbai Published: November 16, 2018 04:15 pm
  • 0

Justin Bieber and Hailey Baldwin's wedding has come as a major shock to many fans given how quickly it happened. Justin and Hailey reconciled in May 2018 and a month later, the No Brainer singer proposed to the Drop The Mic host, in the Bahamas. September 13, 2018, is the reported wedding date for the young power couple as the pair was spotted outside a New York courthouse on the same day. Justin and Hailey do plan a big wedding with their friends and families, as well, in Ontario, Canada.

A few days back, People had reported that Justin is planning to take an extended break from making new music so as to spend time with his ladylove. But one wonders if Hailey is fine with Bieber's decision. According to HollywoodLife.com, Hailey supports Justin's decision to take a break and feels that the singer deserves it with all the pressures of being as famous as him. This also gives the couple more time to spend together as Justin could travel the world with Hailey, while she is working. "Hailey thinks Justin deserves a break, but her career is taking off now more than ever, so shes determined to keep working on her modeling career," a source revealed to HL.

 

 

Hailey is not too worried about her man as she is sure JB will get back into music soon. When Justin does decide to make new music, Hailey will be supportive then as well. 

"Justin has all the money in the world and Hailey isnt doing to bad herself. She is working a lot and looking to make her own empire and she loves being the breadwinner right now in the relationship. She loves doing her part since she never wants things with Justin to fall apart because there would be any stress on finances. Its not about his money or his career, it's about the person he is and she loves that person with all her heart. They both work hard and she loves being in a relationship where both are talented enough to make a living and support each other. It adds to their dynamic," the source concluded.

But worry not, Beliebers! We may not get the sixth album right now but Justin is planning to collaborate further with DJ Khaled.

 

After Naagin, Ekta Kapoor comes up with new show Daayan on &TV

Ekta Kapoor has come up with another show of supernatural genre called Daayan and the show will air on &TV. Read on to know more.
Written By Nikita Roy Mumbai Updated: November 16, 2018 04:26 pm
  • 0
Brace yourself as after supernatural series Naagin, Ekta Kapoor has come up with another show of the same genre called Daayan and the show will air on &TV. The upcoming show from the Balaji Telefilms will start airing from this December and it is a mystery horror with a female protagonist named Jhanvi. The plot will showcase that her race against time is not to save herself from the evil but to find the evil spirit and stop it from executing dangerous ploy. She has to unravel the mystery behind the missing children and defeat evil Daayan.
 
Talking about Naagin, the same is currently one of the top shows. The first season was premiered on November 1, 2015, and the same starred Mouni Roy, Arjun Bijlani, Adaa Khan and Sudha Chandran in key roles. The second season premiered on 8 October 2016 and the makers this year came up with Season 3 and the same was premiered on 2 June 2018. This new season stars Surbhi Jyoti, Anita Hassanandani and Pearl V Puri in lead roles. Check out the first look right here:
 
 
 
Talking about Ekta Kapoor, the TV czarina recently launched Kasuatii Zindagi Kay 2 which is the reboot version of 2000s famous serial of the same name. A few months back, she came up with Qayamat Ki Raat another supernatural drama series and which airs on Star Plus.
